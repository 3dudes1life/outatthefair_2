A Community Message

A pause, not an ending.

After 14 incredible years, Out At The Fair® will be entering a period of hiatus.

When Out At The Fair began, our goal was simple: create visible, welcoming LGBTQ+ spaces at fairs and community events throughout California, New Mexico, Oregon, and Alaska. What started as a single event grew into a movement that expanded to more than 10 locations, reached countless attendees, and demonstrated that inclusion and family-friendly LGBTQ+ programming belonged at our fairs.

Over the years, we were honored to work alongside fair partners, sponsors, performers, volunteers, and community organizations who believed in that vision. Together, we created spaces where people could feel seen, celebrated, and connected.

However, the landscape that once allowed Out At The Fair to grow has changed significantly. Across the industry, community priorities, partnerships, sponsorship opportunities, and organizational values have shifted. As a result, many of the pathways that supported the expansion and sustainability of programs like ours no longer exist in the same way they once did.

This is not a decision we hoped to make, nor one we arrived at lightly. Rather, it is the reality of a changing environment that has made it increasingly difficult to continue the program in a way that reflects the mission and standards that have guided us for the past 14 years.

While this chapter is coming to a pause, we remain incredibly proud of what was accomplished. Out At The Fair helped create visibility in places where many believed it would never be possible. It connected communities, supported performers and organizations, and proved that LGBTQ+ inclusion belongs everywhere.

To every attendee, volunteer, sponsor, performer, fair partner, and supporter: thank you. You made this journey possible.

For now, Out At The Fair will enter a period of hiatus while we reflect on what the future may hold.

Until then, continue to be visible, continue to be proud, and continue creating the community that has always been at the heart of this movement.

Be Loud. Be Proud.
William Zakrajshek
Founder, Out At The Fair®
