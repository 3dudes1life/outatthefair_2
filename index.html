A Community Message

A pause, not an ending.

After 14 incredible years, Out at the Fair® will be entering a period of hiatus following our 2026 fair season.

What began as a single event grew into a movement that expanded to more than 10 fairgrounds and community events throughout California. Together, we created visible, welcoming LGBTQ+ spaces where families, friends, performers, organizations, and allies could come together and celebrate community.

This is not a decision we hoped to make, nor one we arrived at lightly. Over the years, the fair industry, community priorities, sponsorship landscape, and organizational values have changed significantly. Many of the pathways and partnerships that once supported the growth and sustainability of programs like Out at the Fair® no longer exist in the same way they once did.

As a result, we have reached a point where continuing the program at the scale, quality, and level of impact our community deserves is no longer sustainable. Rather than compromise the mission that has guided us for the past 14 years, we believe it is time to pause and protect the legacy we built together.

We are incredibly proud of what this community accomplished. Out at the Fair® helped create visibility in places where many believed it would never be possible. We watched families grow, friendships form, performers shine, and countless memories be made. Most importantly, we proved that LGBTQ+ inclusion belongs everywhere.

We are deeply grateful to every fair partner, sponsor, volunteer, performer, community organization, and attendee who made this journey possible. Your support helped create something truly special, and we will always be thankful for the role you played in this story.

This is not goodbye. It is a moment to honor the past, celebrate what we accomplished together, and leave the door open for whatever the future may hold.

Be Loud. Be Proud.
William Zakrajshek
Founder, Out at the Fair®
