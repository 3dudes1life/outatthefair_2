A Community Message

A pause, not an ending.

After 14 incredible years, we have made the difficult decision to place Out at the Fair® on hiatus following our 2026 fair season.

This decision was not made lightly. We are proud of everything this community has accomplished together—from our first event to the thousands of people who have joined us over the years. We have watched families grow, friendships form, performers shine, and countless memories be created.

As the fair industry continues to evolve, so have we. The values, priorities, and realities of producing community events have changed significantly, and we believe this is the right time to pause, reflect, and protect the heart of what made Out at the Fair® special.

We are deeply grateful to every fair partner, sponsor, volunteer, performer, community organization, and attendee who helped make Out at the Fair® possible. Your support allowed us to create something many people once believed could never exist.

This is not goodbye. It is a moment to celebrate what we built together, honor the legacy, and look toward whatever comes next.

Be Loud. Be Proud.
William Zakrajshek
Founder, Out at the Fair®
