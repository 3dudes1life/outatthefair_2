More than a themed day

A home base inside the full fair experience.

Out at the Fair® creates a visible place of connection without separating the community from everything that makes a fair special. Guests can explore the midway, food, exhibits and entertainment while always knowing there is a stage and space built for them.

It is a triangle of support: the fair, the community and Out at the Fair working together to make belonging part of the entire day.
