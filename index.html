Welcome to Out at the Fair

Pride belongs everywhere—even at the fair.

Out at the Fair® is a one-of-a-kind event that celebrates diversity and inclusion in a joyful fairground setting. Each event brings together entertainment, community, food, games and all-ages experiences.

Our goal is simple: create a visible, welcoming space where LGBTQ+ people, families, friends and allies can enjoy the entire fair while always knowing there is a place built for connection, celebration and belonging.
