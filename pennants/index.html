Our Pennants

The colorful pennants at Out at the Fair were created to make many parts of the LGBTQ+ community visible throughout the event space.

Community flags

Every color makes the fair brighter.

Our pennants are a visual reminder that LGBTQ+ community is not one single story. The collection has evolved over time and can continue expanding as language, flags and community needs grow.

Progress LGBTQ+ visibility

Celebrating the broader LGBTQ+ community with visibility for marginalized queer people and the transgender community.

Bisexual LGBTQ+ visibility

Visibility and belonging for people who experience attraction to more than one gender.

Pansexual LGBTQ+ visibility

Celebrating attraction that is not limited by gender.

Transgender LGBTQ+ visibility

Visibility, dignity and joy for transgender and gender-diverse people.

Genderqueer LGBTQ+ visibility

Recognizing people whose gender identity exists outside conventional expectations.

Bear Brotherhood

Celebrating bear community, body diversity and connection.

Non-Binary LGBTQ+ visibility

Visibility for people whose gender is not exclusively male or female.

Lesbian LGBTQ+ visibility

Celebrating lesbian identity, community and love.

Intersex LGBTQ+ visibility

Supporting intersex visibility, wholeness, autonomy and human rights.

The official LGBTQ+ family-friendly festival and community celebration of the fair industry.
