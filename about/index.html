About Out at the Fair

LGBTQ+ celebration without leaving the fair behind.

Out at the Fair® is a family-friendly LGBTQ+ festival brand and fair-industry program produced by OutAt Inc.

Our mission

Create a place where people know they belong.

OATF brings visible LGBTQ+ programming into mainstream fair settings through live entertainment, community partners, family activities, games, giveaways and a welcoming stage guests can return to throughout the day.

Our model

Embedded, not isolated.

The experience is designed as part of the host fair. Guests enjoy the entire fairgrounds rather than entering a separate LGBTQ+ venue disconnected from the larger event.
