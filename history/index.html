Our history

From one fair day to a lasting tradition.

Out at the Fair began in 2011 as an unofficial gathering at the San Diego County Fair and grew into an official, award-winning LGBTQ+ fairground experience.

Why it started

Visibility inside everyday public life.

The idea was never to build another separate LGBTQ+ celebration festival. It was to create a welcoming home base inside a mainstream county fair, where LGBTQ+ guests could enjoy the same rides, food, exhibits, concerts and traditions as everyone else while also having a stage and community space designed with them in mind.

That model turned an unofficial gathering into a formal partnership with fairgrounds, local organizations, performers and community allies.
